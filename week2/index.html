Hi, I'm Lizzie Su! I'm a rising 4th year studying Geophysics with a minor in Geospatial Information Systems & Technology at UCLA.

My goal as a critical cartographer is to generate awareness of systemic violence and build interest in radical movements by forcing viewers to question their assumptions about where they live and work. Land has never been just an economic resource or simply a background for display. What does it really mean to ask where?
